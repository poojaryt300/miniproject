About Smart TVs

> Smart TVs are modern televisions that connect to the internet and provide online streaming services like YouTube, Netflix, and Prime Video.They allow users to watch movies, shows, live videos, and online content easily from different applications. Smart TVs also support screen mirroring, Wi-Fi connectivity, and built-in apps for entertainment and communication

Latest Smart TVs support 4K resolution, voice control, gaming features, and high-quality sound systems for better entertainment.
