About AI

Artificial Intelligence helps machines think and learn like humans.AI is used in chatbots, self-driving cars, healthcare, and education.It helps computers perform tasks such as problem-solving, speech recognition, image processing, and decision-making. AI technology improves efficiency, saves time, and reduces human effort in many industries. Today, Artificial Intelligence is becoming an important part of modern technology and everyday life.
