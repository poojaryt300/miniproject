About Laptops

Laptops are portable computers used for studying, gaming, office work,programming, and multimedia editing.They provide high performance, fast processing speed, and large storage capacity for different tasks. Modern laptops come with advanced features such as powerful processors, high-quality displays, long battery life, and lightweight designs. Laptops are widely used by students, professionals, and businesses for communication, online learning, entertainment, and productivity
